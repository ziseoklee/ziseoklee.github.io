Optimal Transport

Abstract

The study of optimal transport focuses on the most efficient ways to move and allocate resources. It has significant implications in various fields, including economics, logistics, and machine learning. Here, we explore foundational concepts, mathematical formulations, and practical applications of optimal transport theory in the field of generative modeling.

References
