Weekly Assignments

    Each week, you are required to read the chapters assigned for that week and write a short paragraph discussing what you have read. In you paragraph, be sure to incude the following:

  • Your understanding of the material (Summarize what you learned in your own words)
  • Questions, counterclaims, or topics for discussion (At least one)
  • Changes in your life (Observe the chnages that result from exercising discipline that week)
