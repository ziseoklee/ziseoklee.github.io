I was born on August 28, 2003. August 28 is usually written as 8/28, which can be mistaken for 8128! (with "new eyes") 8128 is the fourth perfect number - a number equal to the sum of its positive proper divisors.

8128 = 64 * 127 = 1 + 2 + 4 + 8 + 16 + 32 + 64 + 127 + 254 + 508 + 1016 + 2032 + 4064

Also, 8128 is the 11th harmonic divisor number, a happy number, the 64th hexagonal number, the 127th triangular number, the 8th 292-gonal number, the fourth 1356-gonal number, and the 43rd centered nonagonal number.
