Biography

Ziseok Lee is a graduate student in the Department of Biomedical Sciences at Seoul National University. He is a member of the Artificial Intelligence and Biomedical Informatics Lab (AIBL) under the supervision of Professor Kyungsu Kim. He received his B.S. degree in the Department of Mathematical Sciences with a double major in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Seoul National University in 2025. His research interests include probabilistic generative models (diffusion models, schrodinger bridges, stochastic interpolants) and their applications to biomedical data (drug discovery, molecule design).
