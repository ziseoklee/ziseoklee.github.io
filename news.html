Paper Accepted to ICCV 2025

June 2025

Our paper on instruction-guided image editing, Early Timestep Zero-Shot Candidate Selection for Instruction-Guided Image Editing, has been accepted to ICCV 2025 (2023 CS/AI-Conference Rank 2nd, Impact Score of 43.8, 2024 Google Scholar Top Publication Rank 2nd for Computer Vision & Pattern Recognition (13th for Global)). Huge congratulations and thanks to Joowon Kim for leading this work as co-first author!

Paper Accepted to GenBio @ ICML Workshop 2025

June 2025

Our latest molecular generation AI research, HybridLinker: Topology-Guided Posterior Sampling for Enhanced Diversity and Validity in 3D Molecular Linker Generation, has been selected for presentation at the ICML GenBio Workshop 2025 — a premier global forum on Generative AI for Biology, featuring participation from Nobel Laureate David Baker. Huge congratulations to Minyeong Hwang for leading this effort!
