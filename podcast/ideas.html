Podcast Ideas

Definition

Story

A Story is transcendence of limitation.
A great story has the aspect of distillation of interesting experience. It captures the essence of profound character transformation while leaving out the boring and mundane details.

Archetype

The Innocent, Everyman, Hero, Outlaw, Explorer, Creator, Ruler, Magician, Lover, Caregiver, Jester, and Sage

"But the fact that you have limitations means that the plot of your life is the overcoming of those limitations. If you didn't have limitations well there wouldn't be a plot and maybe there would be no life. So that's part of the reason why perhaps you have to accept the fact that you're flawed and insufficient and live with it and consider it a precondition for being. It's at least a reasonable idea."

The greatest motivator is the truth

In poarticular, your own vulnerabilities, failures, and hidden actions - genuine weakness within you - that reveal itself only when you choose to be honest with yourself. (Or you avoid them for too long)
The meaning of life is then derived from the transcendence of such wretched, dark self into the light which not only helps you but also everyone around you. And here is where you meet God.
