SPAW Test

The SPAW test developed by Zi-Seok Lee and Seong Kyun Jung is a
reinterpretation of the MIMARA and ROCI-II tests using
Topological Data Analysis.

Your romantic relationship is classified into 4 categories:
Stable, Pleaser, Affection Seeker, and Withdrawer.

We also provide results and detailed descriptions of your
attachment style, conflict resolution style, and big five traits.
